WIT VR EXHIBITION**

** Women and non-binary people in tech virtual reality exhibition

The project

This project is about making an online exhibition aiming to give visibility to women and non-binary people in tech and in computer science. Very often their contributions are overlooked or forgotten.

That's why we want to explore possibilities in VR and in-browser 3D with JavaScript and aframe.io to exhibit the life, work, struggles and successes of these women and non-binary people.

Who are we?

We are students from the university HTW Berlin. We have a limited time to bring this project to life. It will be very experimental and we hope it will be fun. Stay tuned :D

How can you help?

Our goal is to find an intersectional group of women and non-binary people in computer science to portrait. If you know a person that we should portrait please contact us and tell us the name and if possible a link with more information.
