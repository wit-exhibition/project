WIT VR EXHIBITION**

** Women* in tech virtual reality exhibition

The project

This project is about making an online exhibition aiming to give visibility to women* in tech and in computer science. Very often their contributions are overlooked or forgotten.

That's why we want to explore possibilities in VR and in-browser 3D with JavaScript (e.g. three.js, aframe.io or WebGL) to exhibit the life, work, struggles and successes of these women*.

Who are we?

We are students from the university HTW Berlin. We have a limited time to bring this project to life. It will be very experimental and we hope it will be fun. Stay tuned :)

How can you help?

Our goal is to find a intersectional group of women* in computer science to portrait. If you know a person that we should portrait please contact us and tell us the name and if possible a link with more information.
